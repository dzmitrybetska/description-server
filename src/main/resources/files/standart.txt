The "standard" room has a double bed or two single beds, a desk and a coffee table, a chair, two easy chairs, a TV, a refrigerator on the nightstand, a wardrobe and a telephone. In the bathroom there is a hair dryer, hygiene products, a set of towels. The room has been redecorated, the bathroom is equipped with modern fixtures, the bed is made with high-quality bedding from Turkey. The average room size is 16-22 square meters. m. and is intended mainly for one or two guests.
